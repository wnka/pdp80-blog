I was using Claude Code to make some style tweaks to this blog, and happened to notice that my first post was from May 2006. That's crazy.

This started as a WordPress blog, then I migrated to OctoPress, and finally Hugo. This weekend I almost migrated to Zola, but instead I turned Claude Code loose on fixing some appearance things I didn't like and following Hugo patterns better. The thing I don't like about Hugo is they randomly break stuff, but with Claude I can just get it to fix it.

Thinking back what my life was like 20 years ago... I was living in Aurora, CO working at a job a didn't like and had been dating my girlfriend for about a year. November 1st 2006 is when she and I moved to Seattle so that I could work on the SQL Server team at Microsoft, and now I've lived in Seattle longer than any other place. Wild. Now we've almost got a 1st grader!

Looking back on other posts, I got my Astro City arcade cab in May of 2011, so fifteen years ago. Geez.

I always mean to write more here, but as you can see over the last 20 years I write in bursts when I'm geeking out about something. The different eras:
1. Gawker - I wrote this open source Mac Timelapse app as a way to learn Objective-C and have fun. I had to apply to SourceForge for code hosting using CVS for version control. I pretty much abandoned it after a bunch of the Carbon frameworks I was using for video capture got deprecated and the initial Cocoa versions of those APIs sucked. Plus, I got a better job.
2. The shmups era - Arcade cabs, PCBs, vertically oriented monitors. I still love those games but they're a lot of work. I've actually thought about selling off my cabs and PCBs as emulation has gotten a lot better. I might try to play something seriously and see if it still sparks joy.
3. Biking era - Pre-kid I could go on long bike rides. I still could, but I don't. I do ride an e-bike to work everyday but that doesn't really count. Biking around Seattle, even if it is e-biking, is by far my favorite way to get around.
4. Random whatever whatever era - JRPGs! Non-Shmup games! Music! Movies!

I feel like I'll always have a blog and write about random stuff. Exciting!
